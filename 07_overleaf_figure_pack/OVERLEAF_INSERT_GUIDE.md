# Overleaf Insert Guide

1. Upload `figures_png/` or `figures_pdf/` to your Overleaf project.
2. Use `FIGURE_CAPTIONS_CN.md` as the source of standardized Chinese figure captions.
3. Use `tables/` as the source for manuscript tables or convert them to LaTeX tables.
4. Recommended policy: use PDF in final manuscript for schematic/composite figures and PNG for screenshots/heatmaps if needed.
